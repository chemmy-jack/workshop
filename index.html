CKMUN2020

How was your first MUN experience? Did you tremble with excitement while addressing your statement? Or did you mesmerize other delegates by your impressive speech? Try to recall the scene when you approached the podium, grabbed the microphone, and forced every single word out.

A year ago, we took part in the Model United Nations community. The reason we chose to attend MUN conferences is not merely to have a chance to dressing in formal attire or dance during banquets, but to learn and grow from every experience.

In the year, we had participated in conferences across Taiwan and had learned to be competent delegates. Yet, we recognized a phenomenon that the core value of Model UN, to train speaking, debating, and writing skills in addition to critical thinking, teamwork, and personal charisma, has been depreciating.

Therefore, we came up with holding CKMUN, aiming to recreate and fulfill the value of Model UN. We strive to make the MUN experience as best as it could be. We try to come up with something different not because it’s easy, but because it’s hard. However, we can not do it alone. If you are an experienced MUNer or someone who is good at public speaking and critical thinking, we are honored to invite you to join us. Together, we can build a memorable conference, and more importantly, fulfill the passion we all have for model UN together!
